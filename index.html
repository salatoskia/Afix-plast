Top quality, without compromise

The company Afix Plast Ltd was established with 100% private capital in 2003 in Prilep, Republic of Macedonia. It is engaged in the production of fitting parts for home drainage and sewerage made ​​of plastic. Its facilities cover 4000 m2 open space and 2200 m2 indoor space of which 1200 m2 is a warehouse for raw and finished product and 1000 m2 production hall where it is operated with 10 injection machines, Krauss Maffei and Battenfeld.
